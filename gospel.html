Salvation

Elements of Salvation

What should True Salvation be made of ?

True Salvation should be made of repentance and total faith in God


What is repentance?

Repentance is a heartfelt sorrow for sin, a renouncing of it, and a sincere commitment to forsake it and walk in obedience.
Isa 55:6-7
6 Seek ye the LORD while he may be found, call ye upon him while he is near: 7 Let the wicked forsake his way, and the unrighteous man his thoughts: and let him return unto the LORD, and he will have mercy upon him; and to our God, for he will abundantly pardon.


What should true repentance lead to?

True repentance must lead to:
Conviction of sin – Acts 2:37 – 38; 19:16 – 19
Confession of sin – Matt 3:6
Forgiveness of sin – Luke 24:46 – 47, Acts 2:38
Turning to God – Acts 3:19
Faith (Believing in God) – Acts 20:21
Turning from sin – 2 Chro. 6:26
Changed lifestyle – Mat. 3:2-8


How to repent and receive Jesus Christ as Lord and personal Saviour?

1. Admit you are a sinner.
2. Be willing to turn from your sins (repent)
3. Believe that Jesus Christ died for you on the cross and rose from the grave.
4. Through prayer, invite Jesus Christ into your heart and control your life through the Holy Spirit
(Receive Him as Lord and Personal Saviour).

Sinners' Prayer one


Dear Lord Jesus, I know that I am a sinner, and I ask for forgiveness. I believe you died for my sins and rose from the dead. I turn from my sins and invite you to come into my heart and life. I want to trust and follow you as my Lord and Saviour. In your Name. Amen


Sinners’ Prayer two

Dear God, I know that I sinned by breaking your laws and I ask for forgiveness. I believe that Jesus Christ died for my sins. I want to be born again and receive new life in Him. I will follow Jesus as my Lord and obey Him in all that I do. In the name of Jesus, I pray. Amen


Two Ingredients for Salvation

  • Belief in the death, burial and resurrection of Christ Jesus.
  • Confess Jesus Christ as Lord and Personal Savior.
