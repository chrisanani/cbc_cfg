DIRECTION

The group has gone through many transformations. One of its paramount concerns has been to have a group whose members are fully committed to the Lord, demonstrated by holy living and full participation in the Groupâ€™s activities. Our focus can be summarised below.

  1. To provide counselling for new converts who come to accept Christ Jesus as Lord and Saviour.

  2. To counsel and help people who make other decisions such as rededication, transfer of membership, joining the church, assurance of Salvation etc.

  3. To help establish all people counselled by the group.

  4. To foster Christian fellowship, spiritual growth and Christian maturity among counselees.

  5. To serve as a link between the church and new converts, especially baptised church members.

  6. To serve as a prayer force for new converts.

  7. To organize training programs on counselling and Follow-up and related topics for members in particular and the church in general.

  8. To serve as a fellowship for Christian maturity and growth for members of CFG.
