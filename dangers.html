Some dangers to avoid in counseling

  1. Over reliance on one sided information.

  2. Jumping to premature conclusions.

  3. Over involvement. You stand to harm yourself if you take over counselee’s problems.

  4. Getting closely associated with the opposite sex.

  5. Taking undue advantage of counselee [especially if they have something you need or don’t have].

  6. Releasing classified information during unguarded moments. Listen more, talk less.

  7. Over spiritualizing and / or under spiritualizing issues.

  8. Spiritual contamination especially where due to your prayerlessness you digest the filth the counselee pours out as he/she confesses.
