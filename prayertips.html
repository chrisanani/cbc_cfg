PRAYER FOR COUNSELEES

Phil 1:2-6
2 Grace be unto you, and peace, from God our Father, and from the Lord Jesus Christ.
3 I thank my God upon every remembrance of you,
4 Always in every prayer of mine for you all making request with joy,
5 For your fellowship in the gospel from the first day until now;
6 Being confident of this very thing, that he which hath begun a good work in you will perform it until the day of Jesus Christ:

  • Thank God for your counselees. Mention their names.
  • Pray that your counselees will thirst and be hungry for holiness, righteousness, etc.
  • Pray that they will love God and be in obedience to his word.
  • Pray for God's protection & provision for them and their families.
  • Pray to God to meet the spiritual, physical and emotional needs of your counselees.
  • Pray for the health needs of your counselees.
  • Pray and break any ungodly soulties in the lives of your counselees.
  • Pray and break any ancestral curses in the lives of your counselees.
  • Pray that your counselees will find their spiritual gifts, develop and deploy them.
  • Pray against spiritual attacks directed at your counselees.
  • Pray the marriages of your counselees.
  • Pray for the counselees who are singles.
  • Pray for the families of your counselees to come to the saving faith of Jesus Christ if they don't know Him already.
  • Pray for the occupation for your counselees.
  • Pray that your counselees will not compromise on principles of God.
  • Pray that your counselees will fear the Lord always.
